About Green Habbit

Leading the solar revolution in Madhya Pradesh

Our Story

Green Habbit was founded with a vision to make solar energy accessible to everyone in Madhya Pradesh. Starting from Indore, we have expanded our services to Mhow and now Shivpuri, bringing clean energy solutions to both urban and rural areas.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable and efficient solar energy solutions that reduce electricity costs and carbon footprint for our customers.

Our Founders

Green Habbit was co-founded by Mukesh Parashar and Akash Parashar who envisioned a greener future for Madhya Pradesh through sustainable energy solutions.
